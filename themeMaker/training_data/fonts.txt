## Font Usage Guidelines for Widget Design

### For Large Time Displays
- **HelveticaNeue-UltraLight** - Ultra-minimal, modern
- **AvenirNext-UltraLight** - Geometric, clean
- **AppleSDGothicNeo-UltraLight** - Soft, rounded
- **DINCondensed-Bold** - Industrial, condensed
- **Futura-Bold** - Geometric, strong

### For Secondary Information
- **HelveticaNeue-Light** - Subtle, clean
- **AvenirNext-Medium** - Balanced, readable
- **AppleSDGothicNeo-Light** - Soft, approachable
- **Futura-Medium** - Geometric, modern

### For Technical/Digital Aesthetics
- **Menlo-Bold** - Monospace, code-like
- **CourierNewPS-BoldMT** - Typewriter, retro
- **DINAlternate-Bold** - Industrial, technical

### For Friendly/Approachable Designs
- **ArialRoundedMTBold** - Rounded, friendly
- **GillSans** - Humanist, warm
- **MarkerFelt-Thin** - Handwritten, personal

### For Luxury/Premium Feel
- **AvenirNext-Heavy** - Sophisticated weight
- **HelveticaNeue-CondensedBlack** - Strong, premium
- **Futura-CondensedExtraBold** - Geometric luxury

## Font Pairing Suggestions

### Modern Minimal
- **Primary**: HelveticaNeue-UltraLight (time)
- **Secondary**: HelveticaNeue-Light (date/info)

### Geometric Contemporary  
- **Primary**: AvenirNext-Bold (time)
- **Secondary**: AvenirNext-UltraLight (secondary info)

### Industrial/Technical
- **Primary**: DINCondensed-Bold (time)
- **Secondary**: Menlo-Regular (details)

### Soft/Organic
- **Primary**: AppleSDGothicNeo-UltraLight (time)
- **Secondary**: AppleSDGothicNeo-Light (info)
