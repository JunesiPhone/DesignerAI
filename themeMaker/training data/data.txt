## Notes for AI Training

1. **Dynamic vs Static**: Most fields return live data from the device
2. **Format Awareness**: The class respects 12/24-hour format settings and language localization
3. **Relative Dates**: Extensive support for dates relative to today (±7 days)
4. **Weather Integration**: Comprehensive weather data including current, hourly, and 5-day forecasts
5. **System Integration**: Deep integration with device status, connectivity, and app data
6. **Customization Focus**: Designed for flexible widget creation with granular data access
7. **Usage**: "data" : "[textHour]"
